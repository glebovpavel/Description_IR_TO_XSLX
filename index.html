GPV Interactive Report to Excel

Getting result of your Interactive Report in Excel.

About

The "GPV Interactive Report to MSExcel" APEX plugin lets you getting results of your Interactive Report in Excel-format, with correct displaying of Strings, Number and Dates.

Many features of Interactive Reports are supported:

  • Filtering and Sorting
  • Control Break
  • Computations
  • Aggregations

    • The minimum required APEX-version is 4.2.
